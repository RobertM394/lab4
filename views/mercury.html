Mercury

• Mercury is the closest planet to the sun.
• A year on Mercury is only 88 Earth days long, due to its proximity to the sun and smaller orbit. However, Mercury turns slowly, with a full day on Mercury lasting 176 Earth days. Mercury's day is longer than its year.
• Although the closest planet to the sun, Mercury is the second-hottest planet in the solar system, with Venus being hotter due its atmosphere.
• Mercury's gravity is 38% that of Earth's gravity, which is too weak to hold a dense atmosphere. Mercury's atmosphere is mostly blown away by solar winds (charged particles flowing from the sun), but Mercury does have a thin atmosphere. Though Mercury is close to the sun, its surface is still relatively cool enough to be solid.
