Earth

The Earth is the third planet from the sun. It is still the only known planet in the universe to have liquid water on its surface. However, Jupiter's moons Europa and Enceladus also have water below a crust of ice. The Earth also possesses an atmosphere of oxygen, nitrogen, carbon dioxide, and other compounds, and is is the only known planet in a solar system's habitable zone to also have water and oxygen.
